About Dr. Maria Aquilina

Welcome to the practice of Dr. Maria Aquilina, Notary Public (Nutar Aquilina) in Malta. With a commitment to excellence and client-focused service, I provide comprehensive notarial services to individuals, families, and businesses throughout Malta.

As a qualified notary public, I ensure that all legal documentation is prepared with the utmost care, accuracy, and professionalism. My practice is built on the principles of integrity, confidentiality, and personalized attention to each client's unique needs.

Whether you're purchasing property, planning your estate, or require legal documentation for business matters, I am here to guide you through the process with clarity and expertise.
