About Dr. Maria Aquilina

Dr. Maria Aquilina graduated as a Doctor of Laws from the University of Malta in 2013 and was awarded her warrant to practice as a Notary Public in the same year. With a commitment to excellence and client-focused service, she provides comprehensive notarial services to individuals, families, and businesses throughout Malta.

Since 2013, Dr. Maria Aquilina has assisted various clients in the purchasing of immovable property, particularly first-time buyers purchasing their first residential property. She also services various private companies in the attestation and notarisation of identification documents required by foreign countries.

Notary Aquilina has extensive experience servicing clients in the drafting and publication of their wills, as well as the preparation and subsequent publication of causa mortis declarations when a person passes away and the heirs need to declare the property inherited and pay the tax thereon.

Her practice is built on the principles of integrity, confidentiality, and personalized attention to each client's unique needs. Whether you're purchasing property, planning your estate, or require legal documentation for business matters, Dr. Maria Aquilina is here to guide you through the process with clarity and expertise.
